Ctrl-Alt-Delete User Stories

Story #1

As a someone who is not very good at typing I want a game that has a range of difficulties so someone from any skill level can play and enjoy the game. The game should also have good mechanics and be user friendly with an intuitive feel to it.

Elaboration: Incorporate different difficulty modes for the player

Constraints: none

Effort Estimation: 2 hours

Acceptance Test: You are able to choose what difficulty you want before the game starts. Easier difficulty has shorer words to type and harder difficulties have longer words to type.


Story #2

As someone who is very competitive I want to see my username and score on a leader board along with other users. This leaderboard should show the top ten best players and the duration of how long they have held that rank.

Elaboration: Implements a leaderboard that keeps track of the high score of other user and shows the top ten best players in the game

Constraints: none

Effort Estimation: 12 hours

Acceptance Test: The leaderboard shows the score, username and amount of time holding the rank for the top ten players and actively updates the list when scores are broken.


Story #3

I'm a kid who doesn't know how to type but would like to learn.

Elaboration: Implement a beginner mode that takes the monster out and gives hints on how to type effectively.

Constraints: This could require refactoring some of the game logic.

Effort Estimation: 20 hours

Acceptance Test: A child could sit down at the computer and learn how to type just using our game.


Story #4

As a player who would like to pause his games and then come back to them really quick I think a pause button would be very beneficial.

Elaboration: I would like to pause the game so I could walk away to do something for a second and then start right were i left off.

Constraints: none

Effort Estimation: 2 hours

Acceptance Test: You could press pause and the whole game would freeze Then when you press play its back to exactly where you left off.


Story #5

As a player who really likes customizing things the way I like them the game should implement custmization features.

Elaboration: I would like be able to choose my own avator or monster. Maybe even build my own character to really make them mine.

Constraints: There should be a good amount of different characters but not to many that it becomes overwhelming

Effort Estimation: 22 hours

Acceptance Test: From the menu you can choose and custmize your character.


Story #6

I am a player who really like to use power ups in games to influence how the game goes.

Elaboration: There should be power ups you can collect as you are running from the monster. These power ups can slow down the speed of the words or they could give you a bigger gap in-between the monster allowing you to make more mistakes.

Constraints: There should only be a few power ups.

Effort Estimation: 14 hours

Acceptance Test: You can run into power ups while playing and it works properly.


Story #7

I am a player who likes second chances. I think the game should Incorporate a health system.

Elaboration: You start the game with three hearts and you can make enough mistakes to run into the moster three times before the game is over

Constraints: There shouldn't be more than three lives unless their from an upgrade or power up

Effort Estimation: 10 hours

Acceptance Test: You can run into the moster for every heart you have and not die until you hit the moster without any.


Story #8

I am a player who really likes to upgrade his character throughout the game.

Elaboration: There should be upgrades you can apply to your character. There upgrades can be stamina or more lives.

Constraints: We would have to create some kind of currency system to buy the upragdes with.

Effort Estimation: 16 hours

Acceptance Test: You can upgrade your character from the menu and it applies to the game.


Story #9

I am a player who likes to challenge myself I would want different levels that I can progress through

Elaboration: There should be a several levels that you need to progress through. Each level could get a bit harder or focus on word using particular letters.

Constraints: none

Effort Estimation: 18 hours

Acceptance Test: You can choose different levels to play through and cant move on to the next until you complete the previous level


Story #10

I am a player who really like to save his game to come back to later

Elaboration: There should be a way to save my progress after i beat a level so i come back to the same point I left off at when i come back to play later.

Constraints: none

Effort Estimation: 13 hours

Acceptance Test: You can save your progress and come back to it later.
